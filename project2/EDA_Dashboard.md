📝 Key Observations & Insights
Revenue Leader: Chair ($195,620) and Printer ($195,613) top total revenue, while Phone ($151,722) lags behind despite similar order volumes.


Mean vs Median Gap: Mean ($1,054) is notably higher than Median ($824), suggesting right-skewed distribution driven by high-quantity bulk orders.


High Cancellation Rate: Cancelled (250) and Returned (247) together represent 41.4% of all orders — a significant business risk worth investigating.


Only 19.3% Delivered: With 231 delivered orders out of 1,200, fulfilment efficiency appears low. Pending (237) adds further concern.

Outliers Detected: 8 orders exceed $3,334 (IQR upper fence). These high-value bulk orders (max $3,456) skew mean revenue upward.


Balanced Payment Methods: Online (258) leads slightly, but all 5 payment methods are within ~12% of each other — healthy diversity.


Instagram Dominates Referrals: With 259 referrals, Instagram edges out Email (250) and Google (241) as the top acquisition channel.



Coupon Adoption: 74.3% of orders used a coupon (FREESHIP, SAVE10, WINTER15). NIL (no coupon) accounts for only 25.75% — pricing pressure is high.


Seasonal Fluctuation: Revenue spikes visible in May 2023 ($63.8K) and dips in April 2023 ($27.8K), suggesting seasonality worth modelling further.


Unit Price Spread: Prices range from $11.39 to $699.93 (std: $197). Mid-range ($300–$500) is the most common price band.
